About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

김경준 임상심리학 박사

캘리포니아 공인 심리학자, Ph.D. • California License #PSY33498

With over 15 years of experience in mental health counseling, I am dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based care to individuals and couples. My therapeutic approach integrates cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness-based interventions, and humanistic principles.

I believe in the inherent strength and resilience of every person. My role is to provide a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings, develop coping strategies, and work toward meaningful change.

Specializations: Anxiety and depression, trauma recovery, relationship counseling, life transitions, and stress management.

Education: Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from UCLA, specialized training in EMDR and Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy.

Our Counseling Services

We offer comprehensive mental health services tailored to your individual needs, providing both online and in-person sessions throughout California.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on your personal growth, healing, and mental wellness. We address anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, and personal development goals.

Couples Counseling

Strengthen your relationship through improved communication, conflict resolution, and deeper emotional connection. We help couples navigate challenges and build stronger bonds.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Evidence-based approach focusing on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to emotional distress and mental health challenges.

Mindfulness & Stress Management

Learn practical mindfulness techniques and stress reduction strategies to improve your overall well-being and develop resilience in facing life's challenges.

Trauma Recovery

Specialized treatment for trauma and PTSD using evidence-based approaches including EMDR and trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy.

Group Therapy

Connect with others facing similar challenges in a supportive group environment. Topics include anxiety management, grief support, and social skills development.

Service Availability

All services are available both online via secure video sessions and in-person at our comfortable office in California. We are licensed to provide services throughout the state of California.
